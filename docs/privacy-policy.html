Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 14, 2025

WhatsApp Micro-CRM ("we", "our", or "us") is dedicated to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome Extension.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data automatically. We only process data that you explicitly choose to "Snap" or save using the extension's interface.

  • Chat Content: When you click "Snap This Lead", the text of the currently open WhatsApp chat is temporarily processed to extract business insights.
  • Contact Information: We store the name of the contact associated with the snapped chat.
  • Account Info: We store your email address for authentication purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for:

  • AI Analysis: Chat text is sent to our secure backend and processed by OpenAI to generate summaries, lead scores, and action items.
  • CRM Functionality: Storing your leads and their details in your personal dashboard.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Supabase, a leader in open-source database solutions.

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/SSL).
  • Access to your data is protected by strict Row Level Security (RLS) policies, meaning only you can access your own leads.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party services to provide functionality:

  • OpenAI: Used for processing text to generate insights. We do not use your data to train OpenAI models.
  • Supabase: Used for authentication and database hosting.

5. User Control

You have full control over your data. You can delete any lead from your dashboard at any time, which permanently removes it from our database.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
